Openclaw

Self-hosted, open-source AI assistant and agent CLI.

  • Self-hosted alternative to hosted AI agents with full data control
  • Ships three binaries: openclaw, moltbot, and clawdbot
  • Shell completion for bash, fish, and zsh included
